My research centers around nonlinear wave phenomena. I am especially interested in mathematical aspects of nonlinear acoustics and the use of analytical and numerical tools in the development of different ultrasonic applications.
Particular topics include:
  • Well-posedness and regularity of nonlinear acoustic models
  • Coupled models of bio-acoustics and elasto-acoustics
  • Error analysis of FE-based numerical discretizations
  • Absorbing boundary conditions for open-domain problems
  • Shape optimization of focusing devices in acoustics
  • Efficient simulation of ultrasonic waves
